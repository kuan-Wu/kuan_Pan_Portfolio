Apple Avatar Icon for School Project

The developer is challenged to illustrate an avatar representing an animal. This is one of the school projects from the Graphics Design class. The purpose of this task is to create 2 Scale-able illustrations that fit for the Avatar icon of Apple devices and the least of the illustration should be more detailed and colorful then the former one.
